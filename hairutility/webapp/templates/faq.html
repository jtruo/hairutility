Website Questions

What is the difference between HairUtility and Pinterest/Instagram/etc.?

The goal of HairUtility is to help people get better haircuts. Stylists can upload complete hair profiles. Users can bring those hair profiles to the stylists and find the right stylist for them.

Will there be a fee for the service?

Yes, I want to be as upfront as possible from the beginning. Assuming the project has many users, there will be a monthly subscription fee per stylist with no hidden costs. The fee will most likely be priced similarly/cheaper than the booking apps. Beta-testers will receive months of free subscriptions. This fee will only exist if the stylists/salons are benefitting from the publicity of many users.

Our hair color formulas are confidential, will we be able to hide them?

It's possible to implement a feature to hide the hair color formulas; however, the hair profiles uploaded by stylists are publicly available. There isn't a plan to hide any information of the hair profiles, but I can implement a feature if it's desired.

Mobile App Questions

Whose phone will be used to create the hair profiles?

Both the stylist or the client can create the hair profiles. Hair profiles created by the stylist will be stored in our cloud server and will be publiclly available. Clients will store the profiles locally using their phone storage.

Will there be an android version?

There will be an android version. I'm focusing on seeing whether both the stylists and people receiving the haircuts are benefitting from the app. The android version will most likely be developed after beta-testing.
