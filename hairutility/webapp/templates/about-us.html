about hairutility

HairUtility is a mobile app and website designed to help you receive better haircuts. Both the stylists and clients can utilitze it. You can find detailed information on all kinds of haircuts and store them onto your phone. The detailed information will help the communication gap. Have you ever wanted a specific haircut, but you didn't know how to describe it? Check here. We're dedicated to putting quality over quantity. We listen to all of your feedback for the implementation of new features.
